          Example 1: Zhihu Operating Data Analysis

          Since the data analysis only needs to be reported in certain Google sheets, there is NO presentation or written report. Therefore, I can only represent part of the relevant codes that perform the analysis.

          When I was an intern, Zhihu was exploring a new business line. My job is to monitor whether the new business line can meet the KPI, how much will this business development cost, and how to minimize the cost. Therefore, one of my jobs required me to use SQL to retrieve daily operating data for managers and help them decide whether we should speed up or just right.

          What's more, Zhihu outsourced part of the business to several other companies, I also need to monitor whether these companies can meet Zhihu's requirements. Therefore, I need to analyze their progress using the R language.

          Here are two samples of using SQL and R to perform data analysis.
